Question:-

What is “InterProcessSignalPropagator”?

 

Answer:-

“InterProcessSignalPropagator” is a “Qt 4” library which helps any Qt developer to achieve following thing:-

·        A “Qt signal” emitted from one process (Qt Application) can be made available in another process (Qt Application)

a.      These two applications may be on same machine

b.      These two applications may be on different machines

c.      These two applications may be on different machines having different operating systems
